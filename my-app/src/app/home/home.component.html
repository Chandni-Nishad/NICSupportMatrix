Digital Transformation

Cyber Security to sectoral eGovernance solutions, National Informatics Centre is driving the Digital Transformation, unlocking the potential of a digital future through a comprehensive suite of Products, Platform & Services.

NIC Products and Platforms

NIC is playing an instrumental role in executing key IT projects, in close collaboration with Central and State Governments, making the last-mile delivery of government services to the citizens a reality, through a variety of digital solutions. NIC endeavours to cater to ICT needs at all levels of governance including central, state, districts, judiciary, and legislative layer.

NIC Data Centers

With the increased expectations from citizens for online services and the number of e-Governance Projects being launched by the Government, the Data Centre requirements are growing exponentially.

Digital India

Digital India is a transformative initiative launched by the Government of India to revolutionize the country’s digital landscape. In an increasingly interconnected world, Digital India seeks to harness the power of technology to empower citizens, enhance governance, and foster inclusive economic growth.
